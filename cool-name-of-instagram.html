Cool Name of Instagram: How to Choose and Make Best Username?

Instagram is the most popular social media platform of today's time where you showcase your thoughts, personality, and creativity. A cool name of Instagram not only makes your account unique and interesting, but also helps in attracting new followers. If you are wondering how to choose a perfect username for your Instagram, then this guide is for you.



What is Instagram Username?

Instagram username is what people see above your profile and by which they search you. It is a unique identity that represents you in the world of Instagram. For example, if you are a fashion enthusiast, then your username should contain fashion-related words. Examples:


  • TrendyVibes
  • StyleIcon
  • GlamGuru

Why is a Cool Name Important for Instagram?

Gives First Impression: Your username is seen first and it creates the first impression of you.

  1. Is Memorable: A catchy and unique username stays in the mind of your followers.
  2. Represents Your Niche: If your username matches the niche of your content, then it is easy for followers to understand what you post.
  3. Perfect for branding: If you are promoting your Instagram account as a brand, a good username helps in branding.


How to Choose a Cool Instagram Name?

Keep it Short and Catchy

Simple and short usernames are easy to remember. Example: SnapSoul, FitVibes.


Make it Unique

Try to make your username completely unique and not a copy of someone else's. Example: PixelPro, NomadLife.


Use Keywords

Include keywords in your username that represent your niche or interest, such as cool IG names, fashion, fitness, or travel.


Use Numbers or Symbols

If your desired username is already taken, make it unique by adding numbers or symbols to it. Example: StyleGuru123, Travel_Diary.


Use Cool Instagram Names Generator

Online tools where you can generate automatic username ideas by selecting a niche.
