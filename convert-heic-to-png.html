What is HEIC File Format?


HEIC is a modern file format that Apple introduced after iOS 11 and macOS High Sierra. Its full form is High Efficiency Image Container, and it is based on HEVC (High Efficiency Video Coding) technology. Its major advantage is that it saves high-quality images in small size.

But one major drawback is that HEIC files do not have universal support. If you have a Windows PC, Android phone, or any old device, then you will not be able to see these images. This is why it becomes necessary to convert HEIC files into universally supported formats like PNG.

What is PNG format and why should we use it?


PNG (Portable Network Graphics) is a popular image format that comes with lossless compression. This means that when you save a PNG file, its image quality does not deteriorate at all.

Benefits of PNG:


Universal support: It opens in every device and software.

  • Transparent background: Perfect for graphic designing and editing.
  • High quality: Image clarity and details are preserved.
  • Converting your HEIC images to PNG means that now you can view and share them on any device without any compatibility issue.
