What is the use of Bra Size Converter?

With the help of our Bra Size to Cup Size Converter, you can find out your perfect bra size by entering your band size and cup size. This tool is not just a basic size calculator but also supports international bra sizes where you can see conversion between US, UK, and EU sizes.


Tool Features:


1. Accurate Bra Size Calculation

This tool calculates the difference between your band size and bust size and gives you the correct bra size. All you have to do is enter your band size and bust size and the tool will instantly give you the correct cup size and fitting.


2. International Size Conversion

If you want to purchase bras from different brands or in international markets, this tool is helpful in converting those sizes as well. With conversions from UK, EU, and Australia sizes, you can easily identify your correct size from globally available brands.


3. Sister Size Calculator

Sister sizes are sizes that are similar in fit but have a slight difference in band and cup size. For example, if your size is 34B, your sister sizes could be 32C and 36A. This tool also suggests possible sister sizes based on your band and cup size, so you can find a comfortable fit.


4. Bra Style Recommendations

Different bra styles are recommended for each cup size and body type, and this tool provides those suggestions for you too. For example, if you are an A or B cup size, you may find push-up or balconette bras more comfortable, while larger sizes recommend full coverage and underwire bras.


5. Size Chart

The size chart lets you easily compare standard band and cup sizes. This bra size chart displays cups with different band sizes, so you can get an idea of which combination may be the best fit for you.
